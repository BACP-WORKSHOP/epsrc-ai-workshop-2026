Workshop outcomes

Funded through the EPSRC Impact Acceleration Account, this workshop aims to strengthen collaboration between engineers and healthcare professionals, identify practical barriers to real-world AI adoption, and generate actionable recommendations to inform future translational research and follow-on funding opportunities.
