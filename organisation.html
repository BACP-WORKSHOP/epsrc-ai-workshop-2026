About the workshop

BACP brings together AI researchers/engineers and healthcare professionals to discuss practical barriers to adoption, real-world deployment, and responsible integration of AI in clinical practice. The workshop is supported by EPSRC IAA to seed cross-disciplinary connections and identify follow-on translational opportunities.

EPSRC IAA objectives

  • Enable cross-disciplinary dialogue between AI and clinical communities.
  • Identify practical barriers and actionable recommendations for translational deployment.
  • Seed follow-on collaboration opportunities (e.g., pilots, grant applications, joint projects).
