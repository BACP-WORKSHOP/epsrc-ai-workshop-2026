BACP Workshop: Bridging AI and Clinical Practice

Date 20 March 2026 Time 09:30–16:00 Venue KCL Waterloo, FWB G.73 Capacity Up to 100 attendees

About

A one-day in-person workshop bringing together AI engineers, researchers, and healthcare professionals to explore practical barriers to AI adoption in clinical practice. Through panel discussions and invited talks, the workshop aims to foster dialogue across disciplines and identify actionable steps toward responsible and translational AI deployment.

Format

Panel discussions • Invited talks • Audience Q&A • Networking

Expected outputs

  • Short statement outlining key barriers and practical recommendations for AI integration in healthcare
  • Identification of potential cross-disciplinary collaborations and follow-on funding opportunities
