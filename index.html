BACP Workshop: Bridging AI and Clinical Practice

Date 20 March 2026 Time 08:30–18:30 Venue KCL Waterloo, FWB G.73 Capacity Up to 100 attendees

About

A one-day in-person workshop bringing together AI engineers and healthcare professionals. The programme includes a panel discussion and talks to explore practical barriers to adoption, responsible integration, and opportunities for future translational collaborations.

Format

Panel discussion • Talks • Round-table style audience discussion • Networking

EPSRC IAA outputs

  • A short workshop output (e.g., statement / briefing note) capturing key barriers and actionable recommendations.
  • New cross-disciplinary connections and concepts to seed future translational activities and collaborations.
